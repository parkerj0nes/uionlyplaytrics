Acronym Definition
NURR
New User Return
users who installed 8-14 days ago and have played in the past 1-7 days
CURR
Continuing User Return
users who have played at least once 8 to 14 days ago and have played at least once again in the past 1 to 7 days
RURR
Recurring User Return
users who have ALREADY INSTALLED and played more than 14 days ago and have since played in the last 1 to 7 days
